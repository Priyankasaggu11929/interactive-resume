Timur's Interactive Resume

How to Play:

Use the arrows to move around and talk to Timur's colleagues. You can find them with the help of the map on the top right corner of the canvas.

Press 'space' to engage in a conversation when positioned right next to the person. Continue pressing space until no more text is being displayed. To disengage a conversation, simply move away.

Press 'backspace' to mute the music.

Press 'F1' to refresh the page and start over.
